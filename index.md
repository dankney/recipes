# Recipes

A small collection of recipes, mostly vegetarian, each with allergen notes and estimated nutrition.

## Mains

- [Gochujang Tofu](gochujang_tofu.md) — chewy air-fried tofu in a sweet-and-spicy Korean glaze.
- [Vegetarian Mongolian Beef](vegetarian_mongolian_beef.md) — plant protein strips in a savory soy-mirin sauce over rice.
- [Cuban Black Bean Tacos](cuban_black_bean_tacos.md) — black beans simmered with peppers, onion, and white wine.
- [Gorgonzola Gnocchi](gorganzola_gnocchi.md) — gnocchi in a rich gorgonzola and parmesan cream sauce.
- [Spicy Gluten-free Mac & Cheese](spicy_gluten-free_mac-n-cheese.md) — extra-sharp cheddar with a cayenne kick and a cheese-puff crust.
- [Vegetarian Tortilla Soup](vegetarian_tortilla_soup.md) — a crock-pot tomato and pepper soup served over tortilla chips.

## Dips & Mezze

- [Creamy Olive Meze](creamy_olive_mez.md) — a kalamata, feta, and sun-dried tomato spread.
- [Mahammura](mahammura.md) — a slightly sweet, spicy roasted red pepper and walnut dip.

## Sauces & Dough

- [Limoncello Cream Sauce](limoncello_cream_sauce.md) — a tomato sauce brightened with limoncello, finished with cream.
- [Pesto Cream Sauce](pesto_cream_sauce.md) — a quick 10-minute pesto and parmesan cream sauce.
- [Bread Machine Pasta Dough](bread_machine_pasta_dough.md) — pasta dough made in a bread machine.

## Sides & Sweets

- [Yorkshire Pudding](yorkshire_pudding.md) — served as a breakfast dish with butter and maple syrup.
- [Lingonberry Syrup](ligonberry_syrup.md) — a simple lingonberry syrup for ice cream and other sweets.
