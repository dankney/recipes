---
title: Creamy Olive Meze
description: A creamy kalamata olive, feta, and sun-dried tomato mezze for pita or vegetables.
---

**Tags:** vegetarian, gluten-free

### Ingredients:

- ~50 large, pitted Kalamata olives
- 2/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes
- 1/3 cup good olive oil



Add the ingredients in order to a food processor, pulsing as you go. Continue the pulse until the meze achieves an almost hummus-like texture. Refrigerate and serve with pita bread

## Allergens

- **Milk (dairy)** — the feta cheese.
- **Gluten / wheat** — not in the mezze itself, but the pita bread it's served with. Serve with vegetables or gluten-free crackers to avoid.
- **Sulfites** — not a top allergen, but sun-dried tomatoes (and some olives) are often treated with sulfites. Check the label if sensitive.

This dish is vegetarian. It is otherwise free of egg, nuts, soy, shellfish, and fish.
